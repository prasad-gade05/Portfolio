# Introduction: My Blogging Journey

A very warm welcome to my blog space. With this blog I begin a new journey of writing about things that happen to catch my interest. First let me introduce myself: I am a 21-year-old who is currently into computers, data, AI and building stuff with a combination of these.

### Why did I start writing blogs?

For the past few years, I have been experimenting with a lot of stuff, building and creating a lot of things and gathering several new experiences while doing so. I feel these experiences shape and define me as a person, define who I am and what I think about and what my philosophies are. For the same reason I wanted to have a platform where I can share all of these experiences, my opinions and my thoughts with people, and what else could be a better place than blogging? I also feel people will get to see a whole new perspective of mine which I feel people less know about. I think writing blogs will also contribute significantly in sharpening my story telling skills.

> Putting it in simpler words, I started writing blogs because I wanted to... as straight forward as that.

### My philosophy:

I have a few simple principles which I will be following while writing all of my blogs:

1. All of my blogs will be a **100% human written** (By me of course) there will be **NO USE OF AI** in writing any of the content
2. Yes, the thumbnails will be AI generated but apart from that everything will be my creativity, my thoughts.
3. I also feel a user should get on a site what he or she is there for and none of the other clutter and for the same reason, all of my blogs will be **to the point specific and will not beat around the bush.**

### Who are these blogs for?

First and foremost, these blogs are for anyone and everyone who is trying to look into things from my perspective. I really feel reading personal blogs could be one of the best ways to understand someone's mind, their thought process and these blogs are for the same purpose, these are for someone trying to know what I did, why I did it, how I did it.

Since I am a Computer Engineer, many of my blogs will revolve around the same theme. Thus there are also a lot of opportunities to learn a new technology, a new tool or perhaps a newer approach to solve a problem or to implement a feature.

### What content to expect?

As I have mentioned, this is going to be my personal _digital garden_ if you'd call it. So this is going to have a varied genre of content from Technology to personal blogs from sports opinions to video games anything and everything which happens to catch my attention and which is stimulating enough for me to write about it.

One day I may write how I built an automated end to end data pipeline which ingests a million records from 5 sources combines them and performs end to end analytics other day you might find me writing about a random but insightful thought which I might have had on a holiday's night by the beach at 3 am. Some day you might hear about ghost buses (yeah something which I recently discovered exist via a friend) if I find it interesting enough.

Additionally I acknowledge the fact that there may be grammatical and/or structural errors in my blogs initially (I will try to my best effort to not have them) but I believe I will improve upon them along the way while writing.

### Blog content structure and style:

1. My blogs will be posted under prasadgade.dev/blogs/ domain.

2. You may additionally access them even via my portfolio prasadgade.dev then navigating to the blogs tab.

3. My blogs section will have some of my philosophies written so as to have a quick onboarding, all of my blogs will be perfectly categorized so as to deliver the right genre to the right people.

4. Additionally each of my blog posts will have a link to my portfolio, my blogs landing page and my GitHub for you to find me on other platforms if you want.

5. I'd personally suggest navigating to my portfolio site would be the better choice since it contains every section you are looking for in an organized manner with quick and easy access.

So with that being said I think I have set a good premise for writing, I humbly welcome you. Let us see how this goes.
